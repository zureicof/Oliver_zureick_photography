Portfolio Overview

This page shows the kind of work I enjoy making the most. A lot of my portfolio is built around sports because sports naturally create emotion, movement, and big moments. I like photographing those moments in a way that feels polished but still real. Some photos are simple action shots, while others are more creative and dramatic. I also create graphics that mix photography with design to make content that feels promotional and professional. That mix of photography and graphic design helps my work stand out. It also shows that I can create more than just single images. I can help build a full visual look around a team, player, or event.

I want my portfolio to show range while still feeling consistent. That is why this page includes baseball, basketball, swimming, and training images along with graphic design examples. Even though the subjects are different, the overall style stays clean, modern, and focused on strong visuals. I want viewers to see that I care about both storytelling and presentation. Good work should feel organized and intentional, not random. This page is designed so visitors can look through different types of content without getting overwhelmed. The button interaction below also gives the site a little movement while still keeping things simple.
