Portfolio Overview

This page highlights the type of work I enjoy creating the most. A lot of my portfolio is centered around sports because sports naturally bring out emotion, movement, and big moments. I like photographing those moments in a way that feels polished but still real.

I also create graphics that mix photography with design, which helps my work stand out. That combination allows me to create more than just single images. I can help build a full visual identity around a team, player, or event.
