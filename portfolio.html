Portfolio Overview

This page highlights the type of work I create most often. My portfolio mixes traditional sports photography with more creative visuals and design-based content. I like making work that looks polished, but still feels real.

The strongest part of my style is variety. I can cover live action, shoot dramatic training images, and also create graphics that help athletes and teams build a stronger visual identity.
