Contact Me

This page is here to make it easy for people to reach out if they are interested in working with me. If you are a coach, athlete, school, or organization looking for sports photography or design work, I want the process to feel simple. I can help with game coverage, athlete portraits, team content, and creative graphics. I think communication is a big part of doing good work because it helps make sure the final product matches the goal. A photography website should not just look nice. It should also tell visitors how to connect and what they can expect. That is why this page includes direct contact information and a little more detail about the type of projects I enjoy.

I also want this site to feel like a real portfolio that could be used outside of class. That means having helpful information that future clients would actually need. Visitors should know what kind of work I create, where I am based, and how to message me. Keeping the design minimal makes those details easy to find. I also included an interactive button below to meet the JavaScript part of the assignment. Small details like that make the site feel more complete. Overall, this page is designed to be clear, useful, and professional while still matching the style of the rest of the site.
